##### Essay
Handwriting is dead and we killed it. Modern technology of technology pushes out writing from the everyday life of the majority of humanity. I firmly believe that it is a negative development.

Years ago computers and nowadays smartphones have reduced previously essential tools such as a notebook, due to increased mobility and broad abilities. It is a matter of seconds when your notes become a letter for a friend or an update for your blog post. 

In addition, typing is the fastest way of note-taking in literally every circumstance, for example, it gives the ability to create lecture notes identical to what the professor said. 

Despite the various advantages of typing, research shows that handwriting notes are much easier to comprehend and learn pieces of information than typed ones.

Moreover, using a brush or pencil helps develop motor skills and concentration development.
