Penumbra is a ring of tissue surrounding the ischemic core that is hypoperfused. This hypoperfusion provides neurons with a chance to recover, unlike neurons in the central core, which usually die suddenly. [[@ferrerSignalingCellDeath2003]]

Both apoptosis and necroptosis occure in grate extend in the penumbra of mice after ischemia.[[@xuanChronicTreatmentWaterSoluble2015]]

Necroptosis occure in penumbra alongside of apoptosis in penumbra in mice during MCAO.
