Title: Necroptosis
tags: #atomic_note  #cell_death


# Notes
Necroptosis is programmed cell death that has morphological features of necrosis. RIPK3 and MLKL are key players in programmed necrosis. LPS, TNF, and Fas could induce necroptosis, however, caspase-8 inactivation could be essential for necroptosis activation.
