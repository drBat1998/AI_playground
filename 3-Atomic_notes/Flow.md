# Notes
Flow state or deep work is the state when you make middle difficult work that you have to and interest to do effortless, sometimes it feels like some sort of magic. 
The flow state reduces the engagement of the prefrontal lobe of the brain in the activity. 
To do it mid-difficulty, clear goals are essential.
