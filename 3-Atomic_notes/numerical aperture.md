Title: numerical aperture
tags: #atomic_note


# Notes
The numerical aperture of microscope objective tells how much light the lens can collect.

It depends on two things:

The half-angle (α) of the light cone that enters the lens.

The refractive index (η) of the medium between the sample and the lens (e.g. air, water, or oil).
