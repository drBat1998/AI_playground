# Notes
[[Noun]] is a part of speech that names a thing. 
[[Pronoun]] is a part of speech that substitutes for a noun if the noun has already been named. 
[[Adjective]] is a part of speech that describes nouns.  
[[Verb]] is a part of speech that describes a noun's action.
[[Adverb]] is a part of speech that describes an adjective, a verb, or another adverb.
[[Preposition]] is a part of speech that the relationships between other words
[[Conjunction]] make it possible to build complex sentences
