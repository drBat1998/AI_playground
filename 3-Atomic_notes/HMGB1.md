# Notes
HMGB1 is a typical damage-associated molecular pattern (DAMP) that can be passively released during various types of cell death, such as pyroptosis, necrosis, apoptosis, necroptosis, ferroptosis, NETosis, and lysosome-mediated and autophagy-dependent cell death.[^1]
