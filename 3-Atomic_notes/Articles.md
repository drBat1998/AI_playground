# Notes
The form a is used before a word beginning with a consonant sound
a man 
a had 
a European
a university
The form `an` is used before vowel sound
an apple
an island
an uncle
an hour

Usage: 
1. Before a singular countable noun that mentioned for the first time. (He bought an ice-cream.)
2. Before a singular countable noun that represent a class of things. (A car must be insured.)
3. With a [[noun complement]]. (He is an actor.)
4. In certain expression of quantitiy ( a lot of)
5. With certain numbers(a half a kilo)
6. In expression of price, speed, ration (a/an = per)(5p a kilo, 10p a dozen)
7. In exclamations before singular, countable nouns
8. Can be palced before mr/mrs/miss + surname
