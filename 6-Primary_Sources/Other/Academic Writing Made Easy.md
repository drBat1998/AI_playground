# Unit 1 Rhetorical Preferences: Meeting the expectations of your target group
It is the writer responsibility to provide any essential prior knowledge and structure the text in a way the audience will get it. 

Each part of writing should be writen into tree parts: introduction, main paragraph, conclusion.

Credibility is influenced by level of formality, avoiding arrogance in tone, and giveing credibility to fellow academics.

Narrative can help send message not only to logic but to emotion as well. On the other hand narrative reduce credibility, due to some researcher think about it as anecdote and its lack of sientificant.
