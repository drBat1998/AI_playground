# Notes
Length of the Primer
- 18-24
Annealing and Melting temperatures 
- The annealing temperature should be 5 degrees lower than the melting temperature
- Two primers should have a similar temperature
GC content 
- 40-60 %
- GC clamp on 3' prime end 
Secondary Structure
- avoid hairpins, self-dimers, or cross-dimers
