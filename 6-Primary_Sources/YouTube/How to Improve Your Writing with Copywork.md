# Notes
[[Copywork]] is a practice of writing. You chose a bunch of writers you admire and copy them to improve your writing. Find their crucial tools that make you admire them so much. To make your [[own voice]], as with the new discovery in science, you have to combine information from multiple sources and add a small portion of your perspective. You have consciously stolen their tools to make your writing better.
