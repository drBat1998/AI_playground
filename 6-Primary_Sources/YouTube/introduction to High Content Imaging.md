Image acquisition guideline

Plate selection
- glass 
- optical grade polystyrene
- thin (<200 Î¼m)
- low bottom 
- without ribs

Autofocus may fail if:
- Bottom thickness varies a lot
- The plate bottom is not planar
- imaging near the well boundary
- The buffer is low

Humidity control on Zeiss CD7?

Seeding density
- high enough to have statistically meaningful results
- Low enough to segment the cell area properly
- 70-85 % for most applications
- 40-60 % application if the signal on the plasma membrane is important.
