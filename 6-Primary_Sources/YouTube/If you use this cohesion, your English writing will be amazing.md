# Notes
Good [[cohesion]]: 
1. Referring devices: this, the, these, that, those, and it.
2. Refering + summary nouns: this deal -> the proposal, the agreement. 
3. Linking words:
	1. Contrast: however, in spite of this, nevertheless, and despite. 
4. Paired cohesive devices: some -> others
5. Substitution referents: other noted that the deals -> other noted that such deals
6. Synonyms.
7. Repeat key terms -> for the most important ideas.
