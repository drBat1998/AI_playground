# Notes
1. Primer Lenght  - 18-30 nucl. 
2. Optimal Tm = 50-65 C
3. FP and RP Tm within 5C of each other
4. Temperatura during PCR must be near 3-4C lower than Tm
5. GC 40-60%
6. GC clamp but no more than 3 Gs in the last 5 bases
7. Avoid repeats in your primer sequence
8. Avoid complementary sequences within the Primer
9. Avoid secondary structures in target sequences
