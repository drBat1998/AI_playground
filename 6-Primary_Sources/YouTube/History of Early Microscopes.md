# Notes
magnifying glass
compound microscope (some relation with telescope)
1665 - Hook micrographia -> cell in morphological sense.
spherical abberation is a worsening of the picture closer to the corner of the field. small one lens could partially solve the problem.  
Leeuwenhoek's microscope - single lens microscope. 1700
Robert Brown's Simple Microscope 1820
Chromatic aberration: Different colors have different aberrations. 
Schleiden and Schwann 1838 - first real cell theory. 
Abbe 1888 - the best compound microscope.
