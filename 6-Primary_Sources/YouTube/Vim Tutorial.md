# Notes
`d` for delete 
`i` to insert mode
`c` is for change - delete the word and change mode to insert one. 

`u` to undo someting 
`control + R` to redo

`yy` to copy a line 
`p` to pase bellow
`P` to pase top
# How to move
`J` and `k` to move down and up
`l` and `h` to move left and write
`G` takes you to the bottom and `gg` takes you to the top.
`{ and }` to move by blocks of code
`o` to insert mode and move below
`w` move by word forward
`b` move by word backward
`t + symbol` moves before the symbol and is used`;` to move to next symbol.
I could use a number before the key to move up or down 
`:30` Move you to a specific 30 line
`*` to move to the other same word
## Visual mode
`V` to use visual mode

you could use it in more difficult and sophisticated way for example
use `ct1` to delete everything to 1 sign
# Misc

`.` - reran previus comand
`r` - replace the latter
