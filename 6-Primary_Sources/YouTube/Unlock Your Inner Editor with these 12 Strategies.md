# Notes
1. Text transplant - choose what you want to edit and take this piece of information to a new page.
2. The sensory scan - fiction, about senses like smell, picture, and noise.
3. The sessions shuffle
4. The first and last
5. The Godfather Story - find the paper that inspires you, and compare your work with it, maybe you will find something to change.
6. The blank page. 
7. The word cloud - the software could check your work and expose what words, and how often, you use them. 
8. Snow blindness
9. The proxy polish
10. The first look
11. Read aloud
