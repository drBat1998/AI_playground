# Notes
1. The brain is efficient in forgetting stuff. 
2. Overload is a state in which your brain overstimulates by information. If the consumption of information is too high, your brain inevitably experiences overload. 
3. Three tactics
	1. Nearest neighbor pattern - relationship is a value. Find a pattern in the knowledge that you already have that is similar. Analogy. 
	2. Visually shape your knowledge.
	3. Active reframe.
