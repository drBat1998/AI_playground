# Notes
Why op-eds?
1. Physician and scientist voices matter.
2. Help shape policy decisions.
3. It is fun to share your ideas with others.

What is an op-ed?
1. opposite the editorial page
2. an opinion essay with a subjective, core argument.
3. Written in a conversational style
4. 800 words or less

Whta makes a great op-ed?
1. Argument and counter-argument
2. Broad applicability
3. Timely
4. Written with clarity
5. Logical flow of ideas
6. Deeply researched
7. Personal

The classic stracture:
1. Heading - the name
2. Lede - is the most important takeaways
3. Nut Graf - more context
4. Copy - restaite the lede
5. The Kicker

or
1. Introductory hook
2. Core argument ("nut graf")
3. Background, pronledm and prescription
4. Counter-argument
5. Summary
6. "Kicker"

Don't rush and budget lots of time to develop and pressure test your ideas.
The more draftes the better
Read aloud
Get a non-medical friedn to read. 
Summarize your argument in a tweet
