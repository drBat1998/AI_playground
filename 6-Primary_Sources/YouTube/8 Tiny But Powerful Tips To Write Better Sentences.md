# Notes
1. The last word must be the most important. 
2. Use contraction when the meaning is defined before. 
3. Write what you try to say, then shift the word order and remove unnecessary information from the sentence.
4. Don't repeat the sentence.
5. Use word variation. 
6. Limit to one uncommon word. 
7. 2- comma rule. 
8. Remove adverbs.
