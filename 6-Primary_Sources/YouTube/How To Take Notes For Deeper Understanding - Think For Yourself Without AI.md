# Notes
Transcription is a note written without context or thought, word by word, from the original person. 

You should capture information with context and your original thoughts, even more your thoughts are more important than original one. 

[[What questions are good enough to ask]]
Question yourself how the chapter or the article changed my understanding of the topic described in it. How did this reading impact me or my research?
