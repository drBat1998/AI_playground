# Notes
Hook to get reader's attention
- in the middle of the action
- to surprise 
- stats

1. Write a hook about a hat
	Everyone is looking at him, or at his hat. 
2. Write a hook about a spill.
	 I spill something on me every week
3. Write a hook about a dog.
	While walking I heard a roar, I thought it was a bear in the city, but it was just a small dog.
