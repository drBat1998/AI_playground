# Notes
[[Introduction of the essay or article]]
1. The question of the reader is "So what?"
2. The introduction has to start with a common ground with the reader. 
	1. Invert your idea.
	2. What do readers already believe? The idea that they knew to be true.
	3. State it clearly. 
	4. Set up the contrast.
3. Problem 
	1. What is the problem and its consequence?
	2. State the condition: But and However. 
	3. So what test - why your reader should care?
	4. No cost, no problem.
4. Solution
