# Notes
1. Prereading
	1. why do I read this shit
	2. Scan the text 
	3. Make a prediction
2. Reading
	1. circle keywords/underline claims
		1. important words
		2. claims are arguable statement
	2. insert symbols
		1. `v` - I know this or agree
		2. `+` - this adds to my knowledge
		3. `-` - this is different from what I thought
		4. `!` - this surprises me
		5. `?` - I wonder
	3. write in the margins
		1. Short summary
		2. Questions
3. After reading
	1. How did my knowledge change?
	2. What did I learn/enjoy/dislike?
	3. Can I make some connections?
	4. Can I identify the author's main points and claims?
