# Notes
1. Verbal tics - fillers-  identify words that you use more often, probably some of them used by you just to fill the quiet, and removing them will probably improve your sentence. - example: actually, basically, really, given. 
2. Delete doublets. example - various and multiple, think and believe, fully and completely, true and accurate. 
3. Redundant implications 
	1. Redundant modifiers - core essentials, basic fundamentals, free gift.
	2. Redundant categories - large in size, round in shape, and at an early time. 
4. Use not any word, but the word.
5. Negatives and affirmatives.
