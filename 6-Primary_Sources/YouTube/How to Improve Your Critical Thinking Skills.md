# Notes
How to take care of new information
1. Source of information
2. Context - what happens in the world at the moment
3. Evidence
4. Cross-references - if multiple sources duplicate information it adds to credibility but is not always 100 percent true
5. Be open to a different perspective
6. Ask questions

How to take care of your beliefs
1. Identify beliefs
2. Origin of the beliefs
3. Challenge your beliefs
4. Controrary evidence
5. Ingage in critical discussion
