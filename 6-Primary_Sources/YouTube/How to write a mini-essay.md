# Notes
The rules of mini-essay:
1. One idea only
2. Short 100-500
3. References 
Three parts structure as any other essay 
Intro - hook - mystery, clearly, engage 
- make strong statements 
- Inspire emotion 
- question 
- Tell a story (middle of an action )
Body
Conclusion
- powerful 
- Message to reader or future self 


After the draft has been created, 5 min away,look for clarity and flow 


Story notes
Using storytelling to clear the  message
