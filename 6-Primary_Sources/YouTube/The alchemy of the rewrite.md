# Notes
Essay is an attempt to understand some topic, firstly the name used by French mayor Michel de Montaigne. On the other hand assay, is a term translates to “to determine the content or quality of something”, used by alchemist named Paracelsus (the father of pharmacology). 

So, in synergy creation and qualification are important. 

The next section is a step-by-step guide on how to rewrite a draft. (Assay):
1. Make a backup - alway make a backup, you are not have any garantie that your future draft be in sligtest stronger.
2. Find the seeds - identify all the indivisible units of meaning, reshape your piece of writing to one chunk is one idea. 
3. Name your parts - identify and naame each paragraphs.
4. Grasp the essence - find the thesis, look for important, provocative, or unifying idea
5. Rearrange the goo - refind outline based on thesis
6. Write from scratch - convert to the paragraph
