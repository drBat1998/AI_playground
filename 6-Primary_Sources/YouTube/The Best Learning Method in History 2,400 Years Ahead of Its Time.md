# Socratic Method
[[Socratic Method]]
1. Write what you already know about the subject
2. In what way does the text confirm or contradict your knowledge?
3. Ask every statement if it is an assumption, and what evidence the author gives, and try to find counterarguments
4. Create a hypothesis and try to disprove it
5. Engage in dialogue. Test ideas of find new perspectives.
6. Reflect (after reading and writing)`
