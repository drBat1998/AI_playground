> [!quote]+ Note ([Page ](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/2LLZF9UN?page=1518&annotation=9UPMGM2D))
  **Marvese programmed cell death occur in bacteria. First discover in E. coli, the system consist of two main player, toxin and antitoxin. Antitoxin constantly interacts with toxin, supreses the death sentences for the cell initiate by the toxin.**

> [!quote]+ Note ([Page 2](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/2LLZF9UN?page=1519&annotation=B8P9N69S))
  **Some of this systems, like mazEF, are stress-induced. Multiple factors from extreme amino acid starvation to DNA damage can decrease mazE, antitoxin, level and leads to uninterapte suicede controled by mazF, toxin.**
