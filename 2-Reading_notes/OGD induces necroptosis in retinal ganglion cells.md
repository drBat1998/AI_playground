Title: OGD induces necroptosis in retinal ganglion cells
Tags: #cell_death #reading_note 


# Notes

# Summary
OGD increases the expression and modification of RIPK1 and RIPK3 kinases followed by MLKL phosphorylation in retinal ganglion cells. As follows the OGD induce necroptosis in retinal ganglion cells.
