Title: JLX001 regulate necroptosis
Tags: #reading_note #cell_death 


# Notes
Boxwood is a traditional Chinese herbal medicine that exerts the effects of dispelling rheumatism, regulating qi and relieving pain. Researchers have extracted the natural compound cyclovirobuxine D (CVB-D; molecular formula: C26H46N2O) from boxwood. Studies confirmed that CVB-D exerts a significant effect on many cardiovascular and cerebrovascular diseases.

On this basis, CVB-D bis-hydrochloride JLX001 (molecular formula: C26H46N2OÂ·2HCl), which has a molecular weight of 475.6,  was artificially synthesized (the structure is shown in Fig. 1). The double hydrochloride structure gives CVB-D better solubility, which is conducive to drug absorption and delivery.

After the administration of JLX001, the relative expression of necroptosis-related molecules was measured by ELISA, RT-PCR, HE staining, immunofluorescence and Western blotting. The results showed that JLX001 significantly reduced pathological damage and the cerebral infarction rate in rat brain tissues, and the expression of neuronal necroptosis-related molecules was reduced, suggesting that JLX001 may regulate CIRI through the classic RIPK1-RIPK3-MLKL necroptosis pathway.
