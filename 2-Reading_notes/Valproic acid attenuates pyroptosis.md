Title: Valproic acid attenuates pyroptosis
Tags: #reading_note #cell_death 


# Notes

# Summary
Valproic acid attenuates pyrotptosis by decreasing il1b, il18, NLP3, NLP1, and GSDMD levels in context of ischemia, however the acid increase pyroptosis in its absence.
