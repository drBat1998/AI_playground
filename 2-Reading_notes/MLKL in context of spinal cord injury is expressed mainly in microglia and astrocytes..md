Title: MLKL in the context of spinal cord injury is expressed mainly in microglia and astrocytes.
Tags: #cell_death #reading_note 


# Notes
**MLKL in the context of spinal cord injury is expressed mainly in microglia (57%) and astrocytes (34%). In contrast, neuron MLKL colocalization is quite low 2.5%.**
# Summary
In spinal cord injury, on the contrary to the hippocampus cells, MLKL is expressed in glial cells.
