Title: Supplements for SH-SY5Y

[[SH-SY5Y]]
# Notes

SH-SY5Y cells were thawed and cultured in medium consisting of:
	DMEM F12 (DMEM F12, Gibco)
	HEPES
	10% FBS (FBS, Sigma Aldrich)
	1% penicillin-streptomycin(P/S, 10378016, Gibco)
	NEAA, 11140050, Gibco

The following day, medium was replaced with 
DMEM/F12, 
2.5% FBS, 
1% P/S 
RA (10 μM, Sigma)

Thereafter, the cells were cultured in 
DMEM/F12, 
1% FBS, 
1% P/S 
RA (10 μM) for 3 days. 

Finally, cells were cultured in 
Neurobasal medium (Gibco) supplemented with B-27, 
glutaMAX (2 mM, Thermo Fisher Scientific), 
primocin (0.1 μg ml−1, Invitrogen), 
BDNF (10 ng ml−1, Bioconnect) and 
RA (10 μM) for a maximum of 21 d.
