Title: PARG protects against parthanatos
Tags: #


# Notes

# Summary
Poly (ADP-ribose) Glycohydrolase (PARG) protects against parthanatos. Genetic overexpression of PARG leads to protection against parthanatos during excitotoxicity and ischemic conditions.
